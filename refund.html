Cancellation & Refund Policy

1. Cancellation by Client

To cancel your tour, you must inform us at least 7 days in advance of your departure date. Cancellations can be made by email or through your client dashboard.

2. Refund Rules

  • 100% refund if canceled 10+ days before tour date.
  • 50% refund if canceled 4–9 days before tour date.
  • No refund for cancellations made within 3 days of the tour.

3. Cancellation by Best Travel Deals

In rare cases of natural calamities, low group participation, or technical issues, we may cancel the tour. A full refund or rescheduling will be offered.

4. Refund Processing

Refunds will be processed to the original payment method within 7–10 business days.

5. Non-Refundable Items

  • Processing or booking fees.
  • Third-party ticket costs once issued.
  • Partial attendance or no-shows.
